# 隐私政策

最后更新:2026-06-17

感谢使用 NudgeDuck/起步鸭(下称"本应用")。我们将在此说明我们如何收集、使用和保护你的信息。

## 我们收集的信息

1. **你输入的内容**:你在主页填写的拖延事项,以及你点击"再小一点 / 换一个动作"时附带的当前小动作。这些内容会发送到我们的服务器,再转发给第三方 AI 供应商生成回复。
2. **生成的小动作和历史记录**:AI 返回的内容以及你的状态(完成/太难/未完成)会保存在本机缓存,并同步到你的登录账号,用于跨设备访问和账号删除。
3. **账号标识**:使用本应用核心功能需要 Apple 登录。我们会保存 Supabase 用户 ID 和 Apple 登录对应的标识,用于身份认证、免费起步次数、历史同步和账号删除。
4. **崩溃和性能信息**:如果应用崩溃,会发送崩溃日志(不含原始任务文本)到我们的监控系统。
5. **使用统计**:我们记录你点击的功能(例如"开始倒计时"、"我完成了"),用于改善产品。这些统计不包含原始任务文本。
6. **设备和应用信息**:操作系统版本、应用版本、语言。
7. **订阅状态**:如果你购买或恢复 Pro 订阅,我们会通过 Apple 和 RevenueCat 处理订阅状态、商品 ID、权益和到期时间。我们不会接触你的银行卡或付款凭据。
8. **可选反馈联系方式**:如果你在反馈表单中留下邮箱,我们会用它联系你回复反馈。

## 我们如何使用信息

- 用于生成小动作建议
- 用于在本机缓存历史记录,并在你的登录账号下同步历史记录
- 用于改善产品体验和修复问题

## 第三方处理者

- **Supabase**(数据存储和身份认证)
- **OpenAI 兼容 LLM 供应商**(处理你的输入并生成回复)
- **Sentry**(崩溃监控)
- **Google Firebase Analytics**(使用统计)
- **RevenueCat**(订阅和恢复购买)

我们不会出售你的数据,也不会用于跨应用追踪。

## 数据保留与删除

- 你可以在「设置 → 清除本地缓存」中清除本机历史和缓存。
- 你可以在「设置 → 账号 → 删除账号」中删除服务器端账号数据,同时清除本机缓存。
- 反馈表单提交后,即使删除账号,反馈正文也会保留(已与你的标识脱钩),用于改善产品。

## 你的权利

- 随时停止使用本应用,并清除本机历史或删除账号
- 登录后可使用每日免费起步次数；订阅和恢复购买由 Apple 与 RevenueCat 处理
- 通过「设置 → 反馈与建议」联系我们

## 联系我们

邮箱:[nudgeduck@gmail.com](mailto:nudgeduck@gmail.com)

## 关于未成年人

本应用面向 4 岁及以上人群,但我们不有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-17

Thank you for using NudgeDuck (the "App"). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## What we collect

1. **What you type**: the procrastination item you enter, and the current tiny step when you tap "make it smaller" or "try another." This is sent to our server and forwarded to a third-party AI provider.
2. **Generated tiny steps and history**: AI responses and your status (done / too hard / skipped) are cached on your device and synced to your signed-in account for cross-device access and account deletion.
3. **Account identifier**: Sign in with Apple is required to use the core app. We store a Supabase user ID and the identifier associated with Apple sign-in for authentication, free daily starts, history sync, and account deletion.
4. **Crash and performance data**: crash logs (excluding original task text) sent to our monitoring service.
5. **Usage stats**: which features you tap (e.g., "start countdown", "I'm done") to improve the product. No original task text included.
6. **Device and app info**: OS version, app version, language.
7. **Subscription status**: if you buy or restore Pro, Apple and RevenueCat process subscription status, product id, entitlements, and expiration time. We do not receive your card or payment credentials.
8. **Optional feedback contact**: if you leave an email in the feedback form, we use it to reply to your feedback.

## How we use it

- Generate tiny step suggestions
- Cache history on your device and sync it under your signed-in account
- Improve product experience and fix issues

## Third-party processors

- **Supabase** (storage and auth)
- **OpenAI-compatible LLM provider** (processes your input)
- **Sentry** (crash reporting)
- **Google Firebase Analytics** (usage stats)
- **RevenueCat** (subscriptions and purchase restore)

We do not sell your data or use it for cross-app tracking.

## Retention and deletion

- You can clear local history and cache via Settings → Clear Local Cache.
- You can delete server-side account data via Settings → Account → Delete Account, which also clears local cache.
- Feedback content is retained (de-linked from your identifier) even after account deletion.

## Your rights

- Stop using the App at any time, and clear local history or delete your account
- Use free daily starts after signing in; subscriptions and purchase restore are handled by Apple and RevenueCat
- Reach us via Settings → Feedback

## Contact

Email: [nudgeduck@gmail.com](mailto:nudgeduck@gmail.com)

## Children

This App is rated 4+, but we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
